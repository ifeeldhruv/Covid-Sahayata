Case Studies: LGBT Community

The question of LGBTQ arises everywhere. To depict on it in very way possible is our responsibility as citizens. LGBTQ has been a topic of debate for decades now and though certain policies has been taken towards equality for all. LGBTQ community still face backlash, Thus it is our duty as people to decrease these situations and prevent backlash on LGBTQ community. In times like these where we are facing covid-19, it is very important to support these groups for equal priority on the basis of medications and healthcare.

Case Studies: Eyesight 

We are concentrating a lot on how to combat and eliminate covid-19 at this time, and because most people are trapped at home, it is high time we look at other illnesses that may have a longer lasting effect than corona.
Example:- We are constantly watching television shows or conducting research on our laptops, accumulating a day's worth of screen time that may have long-term consequences for our eyesight. As a result, we ask that you all think about additional diseases that might occur and see it through.
