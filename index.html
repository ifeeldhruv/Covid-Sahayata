DOES THIS MAKE A DIFFERENCE ?

This program makes a difference as it helps people to find out about the latest variations in the virus as well as the symptoms. It tells us about the vaccinations available, how many vaccines are left in the facility providing it. It also gives us a overview on different facilities as well as their respective addresses.

Our Mission

  • To extend an provide Equal Information to everyone on Every Level about things taking place in the country on daily basis related to Covid.
  • To promote Vaccination and Precautionary measures to everyone to stay safe from Covid and help the nation to Successfully fight against the Ongoing Panademic with ease and proudly.
  • Ensuring Transparency in Information through Various trusted sources and also helping people through funds and resources that can be made available with our efforts and helping.
